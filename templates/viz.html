  • So what does this do anyway?

  • This tool searches books and ebooks that are
    accessible to the selected college. This includes
    local items; centrally-hosted items available to
    the college; and items in CUNY Academic Works.

  • Hover over the text in the blue bubbles to see
    more details.

  • The "Library of Congress Classification" display
    option shows LC classes for both print books and
    for most electronic books. However, LC classes
    would not be included for items from Academic
    Works.

  • When displaying by "Creation Date", records
    before 1950 are grouped by decade, and records
    before 1900 are grouped by century.

  • For the most part, results from the "Topic"
    display option are populated by top level LC
    subject headings, but for some items, such as
    those from Academic Works, these results would
    include tags that are not LCSH.
